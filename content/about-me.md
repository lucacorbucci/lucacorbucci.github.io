Hi 👋, I am Luca Corbucci and I work as a Postdoctoral Researcher at Fondazione Bruno Kessler (FBK) in Trento 🇮🇹 where I am part of the [Machine Translation Unit](https://mt.fbk.eu/) led by [Luisa Bentivogli](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=P8opGwMAAAAJ&hl=it&oi=sra). I am currently working on Multimodal LLMs, specifically on the training and post-training of Speech LLMs. 

I completed my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Pisa 🇮🇹 under the supervision of Prof. [Anna Monreale](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=bA-rXeUAAAAJ) and Dr. [Nicolas Kourtellis](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Q5oWwiQAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao) in July 2025. 
During the PhD, I worked at the intersection between Artificial Intelligence and Safety. Specifically, I focused on Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning, Model (Un)Fairness, and Explainability. 
However, I am attracted to new technologies. Therefore, I am also open to working on different and innovative topics.

In the last few years, I have led international research collaborations, including a 1-year research internship with Telefónica Research and projects with Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz that led to 3 publications to ACM FAccT.
